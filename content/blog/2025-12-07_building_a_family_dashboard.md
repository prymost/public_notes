My next personal project was born out of spite: building a family dashboard to mimic the [Skylight](https://www.costco.com/p/-/skylight-15-smart-touchscreen-calendar-and-organizer/4000234720) organizer constantly shoved in my face by ads.

I figured if I have a **homelab**, I should be able to self-host something like this. I used **Home Assistant** as a starting point after researching the different customization options.

I now have a draft dashboard running on an old **iPad** on my kitchen wall, and my family finds it extremely useful. The biggest win was when the kids started adding tasks for themselves!

I picked up some hardware during Black Friday to build a proper kiosk, which will be the next upgrade. It's a fun project that makes my homelab so much more useful.
